Canada

Canada is the second-largest country in the world by land area, known for its stunning natural landscapes, including vast forests, majestic mountains, and thousands of lakes. Located in North America, it shares the longest international border with the United States. Canada is a multicultural nation with two official languages, English and French, and a reputation for being one of the most welcoming and peaceful countries. Its major cities, such as Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal, are hubs of cultural and economic activity. Canada is also known for its progressive policies, universal healthcare system, and commitment to environmental preservation.
